1. What is Bagnook and how do I sell on it?
Bagnook is an e-commerce platform for selling bags and luggage. To start selling, simply register as a seller, list your products, and start receiving orders.
2. Is there any fee to register as a seller?
No, registering as a seller on Bagnook is completely free. We only charge a small commission on each successful sale.
3. How will I receive payments?
Payments are securely transferred to your registered bank account within 7 working days of order completion.
4. Do I need a GST number to sell?
Yes, a valid GST number is required to list and sell products on Bagnook in compliance with Indian tax laws.
5. How do I handle returns and cancellations?
Our return and cancellation policy protects both sellers and buyers. You'll be notified immediately, and we handle logistics with minimal hassle for you.
6. Can I advertise my products?
Absolutely! We offer affordable advertising tools to boost product visibility and increase sales across the Bagnook platform.
7. How do I contact seller support?
You can reach out to our 24/7 seller support team via your seller dashboard or email us at support@bagnook.com.
